## The Science Behind Court Vision

Court vision refers to a player's ability to anticipate plays, read defenses, and make precise passes. Using NBA tracking data from Second Spectrum, we've analyzed thousands of possessions to identify patterns. For instance, Luka Dončić's vision score—measured by passes per touch and assist efficiency—ranks in the 95th percentile, allowing him to thread needles through crowded lanes.

### Key Metrics We Track
- **Pass Accuracy Under Pressure**: How players maintain composure when double-teamed.
- **Off-Ball Movement Awareness**: Spotting open teammates before receiving the ball.
- **Vision Range**: The area of the court a player effectively scans per possession.
